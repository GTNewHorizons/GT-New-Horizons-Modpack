# Cape Switching
Have you either donated to the pack, or helped to develop it? Well... <Color id="GREEN">__THANK YOU!!__</Color>

Capes can now be switched between using the `/cape` command in game! Your choice is then saved on the client so you don't have to worry about making the change in every differently world. If you feel you should have a cape but don't, please reach out on the [Discord](https://discord.gg/gtnh)
