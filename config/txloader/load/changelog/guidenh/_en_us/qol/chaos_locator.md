# Chaos Locator
As many lategame players are aware, flying to the Chaos Guardian location is a long and painful endeavour, not to mention the hundreds of chunks generated by getting there!

Chaos Guardians always spawn on <Color id="RED">Chaos Islands</Color>, which generate at x and z coordinates that are both multiples of 10,000 (such as x=10,000 and z=0). With the <Color id="GREEN">Chaos Locator</Color> <ItemImage id="gregtech:gt.Item_Chaos_Locator" />, this is a problem of the past!

Simply using this item will allow the player to enter two numbers for x and z in the GUI, and will teleport the player to (10,000 x X-Coord , 10,000 x Z-Coord), so no more pain (apart from the beast that awaits you there...)

This even works cross-dimensionally!
