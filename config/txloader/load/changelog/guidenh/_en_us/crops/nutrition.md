# Nutrient Points
Under the hood, all crops have a <Color id="GREEN">Nutrient Score</Color>. This determines the speed at which a crop grows. There are a number of ways to obtain nutrient points for your crops and to avoid making them sick, it is always best to ensure they are receiving at least a few of the following bonuses:
- <Color id="BLUE">Hydration</Color> -  Up to __+10 Points__
- <Color id="GREEN">Fertilizer</Color> - Up to __+10 Points__
- <Color id="RED">Sky Access</Color> - Up to __+2 Points__ can be gained by the crops seeing the sky
- <Color color="#FF55FF">Preferred Biome</Color> - Up to __+28 Points__ for preferred biome tags (hot, sandy etc.). If no preferred tags are found, high humidity can give up to __+14 Points__. Values below 50% will give no bonuses, and 80%+ maxes the humidity bonus to 14 points. If your base is poor humidity, you can always check out some of the methods listed in <QuestLink id="AAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAJbw==" />
